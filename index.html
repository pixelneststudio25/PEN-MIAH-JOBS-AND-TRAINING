About Us

Who We Are

PEN-MIAH is a Human Resource firm formally registered in 2008. Our activities are mainly Training and Recruitment. Over the years, we have recruited and trained staff for a wide variety of clients in the private sector and government – from unskilled labour (domestic staff inclusive) to skilled staff; SSCE graduates, undergraduates, polytechnic and university graduates, and senior cadre staff.

Our services include shortlisting candidates for organizations with bulk applications, facilitating interviews, and sitting on interview panels.

Our Services

Recruitment

We recruit a wide variety of staff:

  • Unskilled (domestic staff inclusive)
  • Skilled & SSCE graduates
  • Undergraduates & University/Polytechnic graduates
  • Senior cadre staff

We also shortlist candidates, facilitate interviews, and sit on panels for clients.

Training

We offer a wide variety of soft skills:

  • Time Management
  • Communication Skills
  • Self Awareness
  • Work Ethics (3‑part series)

Custom‑made training for clients, delivered in‑house or at your preferred venue.
