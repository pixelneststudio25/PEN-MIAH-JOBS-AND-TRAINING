About Us

Who We Are

PEN-MIAH is a Human Resource firm formally registered in 2008. Our activities are mainly Training and Recruitment. Over the years, we have recruited and trained staff for a wide variety of clients in the private sector and government – from unskilled labour (domestic staff inclusive) to skilled staff; SSCE graduates, undergraduates, polytechnic and university graduates, and senior cadre staff.

Our services include shortlisting candidates for organizations with bulk applications, facilitating interviews, and sitting on interview panels. We are currently the official recruiters for OPAY (an internet transport company in Jos, Plateau State), managing over 300 contract promoters.

Our Founder & CEO

Esther Nanna Yakubu (BL, PNM) is a Lawyer and Human Resources Consultant. Her specialties include recruiting staff, structuring HR and Administrative departments, developing work policies, job descriptions, and creating departments. With over 16 years of experience in the human resource field (since 2003), she is a Professional Negotiator and Mediator skilled in settling workplace conflicts and matching people with appropriate jobs.
